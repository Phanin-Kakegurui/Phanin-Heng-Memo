Course Description Computer-based information technology (IT) has pervaded nearly all aspects of modern business and society. Regardless of the area of study, technology will likely play a significant role in the future of a student’s career. This is especially true in the business world. For being effective in a future job, it is crucial to embrace technology, at least to the extent that a student can be conversational about its most common uses. This course introduces many IT concepts, from what is a system unit to understand the basis of telecommunications. The course will explore the components of the system unit, such as processor, motherboard, input, and output, and will provide a clear idea of how all of them interact. Furthermore, the course will provide an overview of the main protocols involved in telecommunications and in networking, always creating a connection between technology and its usage in society. Finally, the course will provide basic knowledge about how to create a simple website.
